## About
I am a Genetic Engineering student (3rd year, University of Sindh, Jamshoro) working at the intersection of molecular biology and computational science. My work centers on converting raw genomic data into reproducible, clinically relevant insights — bridging wet-lab discovery with dry-lab programming.

I build end-to-end bioinformatics pipelines, process high-throughput sequencing data, and apply computational modeling to cancer genomics — currently focused on *BRCA1* pathogenicity and hereditary breast cancer.

> 🔬 *"Transforming biological data into scientific knowledge — one variant at a time."*

## Research Focus

| Area | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| 🧬 **Next-Generation Sequencing** | High-throughput data preprocessing, quality control, and alignment workflows. |
| 🔬 **Variant Discovery** | SNP/indel calling, structural variant mapping, and genomic annotation. |
| 🎗️ **Cancer Genomics** | Computational modeling of oncogenic pathways; *BRCA1* pathogenicity analysis. |
| 💊 **Precision Medicine** | Targeted bioinformatics pipelines tailored for clinical-grade datasets. |

## Featured Projects

### 🧬 [In Silico Analysis of BRCA1 Variant Pathogenicity](https://github.com/shabanaanwar-dotcom/brca1-pathogenicity-pipeline)
End-to-end computational pipeline evaluating the structural and functional impact of SNPs in the *BRCA1* gene associated with hereditary breast cancer risk.
* Integrates ClinVar API, SnpEff annotations, and Galaxy Cloud workflows.
* Assesses variant pathogenicity using structural modeling approaches.
* Published on Zenodo — **DOI: [10.5281/zenodo.20571186](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20571186)**

### 🔍 [NGS Raw Read Quality Control Automation](https://github.com/shabanaanwar-dotcom/ngs-qc-automation)
Production-grade automation pipeline for quality assessment and adapter trimming of raw sequencing reads. Developed during intensive training at the Institute of Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering (IBGE).
* Automated FastQC → Trimmomatic → MultiQC reporting chain.
* Designed for reproducibility across diverse sequencing datasets.
